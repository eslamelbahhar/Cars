Managed deals

Key points

Main roles. CSR or Purchaser, FSR, Transfer Agent, BO Team, Finance.

Critical documents. Seller EID and Mulkiya, SAA, BAA, buyer full payment, Transfer Agreement, buyer Mulkiya or Hayaza, CRM entries.

Payment release condition. Transfer Agent confirms steps 6 to 8 are complete before requesting seller payment.

Timeline
Check in . CSR or Purchaser Before listing . Purchaser or Call center After deposit . FSR Before transfer . Transfer Agent After transfer . Transfer Agent and BO Team
Responsibility focus
FSR . Checks points 1 to 5 before booking transfer Transfer Agent . Checks points 6 to 8 before seller payment request Finance . Verifies buyer payment before transfer
Detailed checklist
  1. Seller Emirates ID.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  2. Seller Mulkiya or registration card.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  3. Signed SAA. Name and signature must match the seller name on the Mulkiya.
    Generated and updated by the Purchaser or Call center before listing the car.
  4. Signed BAA. Signed by the buyer, with buyer name as per the Mulkiya.
    Generated and updated by the FSR after deposit is collected.
  5. Full payment from the buyer.
    Collected by the FSR and verified by Finance before booking the transfer. Points 1 to 5 are checked by the FSR before booking the transfer.
  6. Signed Transfer Agreement form.
    If the beneficiary is someone other than the seller, obtain Emirates ID for an individual or trade license for a company account. Generated and updated by the Transfer Agent before transfer.
  7. Buyer Mulkiya or registration card or Hayaza or possession certificate.
    Updated by the Transfer Agent after transfer completion.
  8. CRM updated.
    Marked sold by BO Team after the Transfer Agent shares buyer Mulkiya in the WhatsApp group. Seller and buyer names as per the Mulkiya or registration card. Selling price, success fee, and buyer fee are correct and updated by the Transfer Agent before transfer. Points 6 to 8 are checked by the Transfer Agent before requesting seller payment.

Detailed checklist
  1. Company trade license.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  2. Company Mulkiya or registration card.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  3. Representative Emirates ID. Name must be mentioned on the license.
    Checked and updated by the Transfer Agent at the time of transfer.
  4. Signed SAA by the representative. Name and signature of the person signing must match POA, or the name must be on the trade license.
    Generated and updated by the Purchaser or Call center before listing the car.
  5. Signed BAA. Signed by the buyer, name as per the Mulkiya.
    Generated and updated by the FSR after deposit is collected.
  6. Full payment from the buyer.
    Collected by the FSR and verified by Finance before booking the transfer. Points 1 to 6 are checked by the FSR before booking the transfer.
  7. Signed Transfer Agreement form by the representative.
    Beneficiary is either the company account or representative account. Generated and updated by the Transfer Agent before transfer.
  8. Buyer Mulkiya or registration card or Hayaza or possession certificate.
    Updated by the Transfer Agent after transfer completion.
  9. CRM updated.
    Marked sold by BO Team after the Transfer Agent shares buyer Mulkiya in the WhatsApp group. Seller and buyer names as per Mulkiya or registration card. Selling price, success fee, and buyer fee correct and updated by Transfer Agent before transfer. Points 7 to 9 are checked by the Transfer Agent before requesting seller payment.

In cases where the buyer is a company representative or acts via POA, additional requirements apply. These include company trade license, representative Emirates ID (listed on license or POA), POA, and signed BAA by the authorized representative. CRM buyer information must reflect the details as per the Mulkiya.

Auction deals

Key points

Main roles. Purchaser, Auction stakeholders, CSR, DRE, Transfer Agent, BO Team, Auction TL, Finance.

Critical documents. Auction team email approval, seller EID and Mulkiya, signed VSA, buyer Mulkiya or Hayaza, full payment from dealer, CRM entries including VAT, fees, and highest bid details.

Payment release condition. Purchaser checks all points before requesting seller payment.

Timeline
Auction approval . Email chain Check in . CSR or Purchaser Deal finalisation . VSA After transfer . Transfer Agent updates buyer Mulkiya Before seller payment . CRM fully updated
Responsibility focus
Purchaser . Starts email chain and checks all points DRE and Finance . Verify dealer payment before transfer Auction TL . Highest bid and seller deductions
Detailed checklist
  1. Email confirmation from the auction team.
    If the deal is below the auction criteria, Faizan approval is required. Purchaser starts the chain and auction stakeholders approve.
  2. Seller Emirates ID.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  3. Seller Mulkiya or registration card.
    Collected and updated at check in by the CSR or Purchaser.
  4. Signed VSA.
    If the beneficiary is someone other than the seller, obtain Emirates ID for individual or trade license for company account. Purchaser updates after deal finalisation.
  5. Buyer Mulkiya or registration card or Hayaza or possession certificate.
    Updated by the Transfer Agent after the transfer is completed.
  6. Full payment from the dealer.
    Collected by DRE and verified by Finance before booking transfer.
  7. CRM updated.
    Marked sold by BO Team. Seller name as per Mulkiya or registration card via VSA. VAT status PMS, updated by Purchaser at deal closure. Purchase price via VSA. Seller admin fees updated by Purchaser at deal closure. Selling price, dealer admin fees and dealer transfer fees updated by DRE and then finalised by Transfer Agent in CRM. RTA fees, fines and passing cost updated by Transfer Agent. Seller deductions updated in CRM by Auction TL. Highest bid recorded and, if not, the reason noted in the approval email by Auction TL. All points are checked by Purchaser before requesting seller payment.

Important payment notes
  • Payments must be resubmitted after any issues are resolved.
  • Anyone inquiring about a payment can refer to the daily seller email or CRM for current status.
  • If a payment is marked as verified and the seller has not received payment after 24 hours, contact Finance. It should then be investigated without delay.
